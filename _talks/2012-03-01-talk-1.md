Presented at the Digital Humanities Conference (DiHuCON) 2026 at the University of Alberta. My talk, "A Handcrafted Approach to Digital Recovery," explored the hidden costs of using cloud-based AI tools — from the silent "data tax" embedded in everyday interactions, to the legal vulnerabilities that exist across borders and jurisdictions.
